2020 PTE SUPPORT during COVID-19 Pandemic

Learngaroo Learning in co-operation with AusMandu has decided to offer you FREE access to our LEARNING-PORTAL. We have been building PTE solutions for years and our clients have a 100% track-record. We want you to enjoy the privileges of our clients without being one. We know we are spending hundreds of dollars worth of services without a direct expectation from you just to help you at these difficult times. We believe education should be entirely free, but we are bound to be practical and we need to use resources that we do not control, hence it always comes at the price. The voluntary Jail

We know it sucks. What are we waiting for? We’re just waiting for this to be over. This can be over in many ways, one of which is to expect the situation to nullify all by itself, but it does not seem the case this time. We’re months away from a reliable vaccine that has almost no side-effects and is free of deliberate contamination. All we can do is to use this time effectively, and of course, take care of ourselves and those around us. Let us know what you need and we will try to match resources to your needs.
